Export samples **ABOVE 1 KG MUST** use the e-Sample Web System

1. Receive Email from Customer to IKK Customer Service

Customer service receives initial sample request email from customer with complete details.

Product Specifications:

  • Sample description and product type
  • **GSM** (Gram per Square Meter) specification
  • Grade/quality requirement
  • Quantity needed (number of pieces, kg, rolls, sheets)
  • Size/Format (A4 or custom dimensions)
  • **REMARKS** (special requirements, testing purpose, deadlines)

Recipient Information:

  • **ATTN** - Name of person receiving sample
  • Complete delivery address (street, city, postal code, **country**)
  • Contact email and phone number

Verify Information: Confirm all required details are complete. Acknowledge Receipt: Send confirmation email to customer.

    • 2. Create SRF Form (Sample Request Form)

    Fill out the complete SRF form with all required information from customer email.

    SRF Field Details:

    • **SRF Date:** Enter the date of sample request creation (DD/MM/YYYY)
    • **Prepared By:** Enter the name of person creating the request
    • **Sample Description:** Provide detailed description of the sample product
    • **GSM:** Specify the paper/material weight
    • **Size/Format:** Enter dimensions (e.g., A4, custom size in cm)
    • **Remarks:** Add any special requirements or specifications

    üìã Recipient Details Section:

    • **Recipient Address:** Complete delivery address with postal code and **country**
    • **Phone Number:** Contact number with **country code**

    3. Review and Verify SRF Information

    Double-check all entered information for accuracy. Ensure recipient address is complete with **country code**.

    4. Signature Collection

    Obtain necessary approvals and signatures from department head, QC manager, and operations director.

    5. Scan and Send to QC Team for Production

    Scan completed SRF form. **Save as PDF: SRF_Export_[Date]_[CustomerName].pdf**.

    Email to QC: Send with specified sample preparation timeline.

    6. QC Sample Production Complete

    QC team produces the sample and prepares it for international shipment.

    • **Export Packaging:** QC packages sample appropriately for international shipping.
    • Production Complete: QC confirms sample is ready for export shipment.

    Wait for production completion notification from QC team.

    7. Request Information from General Affairs (GA)

    Obtain essential measurements and shipping costs from GA team after sample production.

    Information Checklist from GA:

  • ‚úì Net Weight (kg)
  • ‚úì Gross Weight (kg)
  • ‚úì Length / Panjang (cm)
  • ‚úì Width / Lebar (cm)
  • ‚úì Height / Tinggi (cm)
  • ‚úì Freight Cost estimate
  • ‚úì Sample size specifications

    • Wait for GA confirmation before proceeding to e-Sample system.

    8. Access e-Sample Web System & Click "NEW"

    Log into e-Sample system and start new request. **Verify sample weight is above 1 KG** before proceeding.

    üîó Direct System Access

    9. Configure Trade Type & Export Settings

    Set up the initial trade configuration:

    • ‚úì **Trade Type:** SAMPLE
    • ‚úì **Export Category:** Khusus Barang Contoh
    • ‚úì **Payment Terms:** FOC (auto)
    • ‚úì **Inco Terms:** DAP (Delivery at Place)
    • ‚úì **Shipment By:** AIR
    • ‚úì **Port of Loading:** Bandara Soekarno Hatta

    10. Enter Country & Customer Destination Details

    Fill in all destination country and customer address information:

    • ‚úì **Country Sold To / Ship To / Destination:** Negara tujuan
    • ‚úì **Product Type:** PAPER
    • ‚úì **Sold To Party / Ship To Party:** Alamat lengkap customer
    • ‚úì **Port of Discharge:** Port negara tujuan
    • ‚úì **Place of Delivery:** Negara customer

    11. Fill Vessel, Schedule & Special Instructions

    Complete shipping vessel information and timeline details:

    • ‚úì **Vessel/Airplane:** KMILE
    • ‚úì **Ship on Board:** 7 hari setelah hari ini
    • ‚úì **Vessel Number:** 8k0801
    • ‚úì **Special Instruction:** From SRF (copy remarks/notes)

    12. Calculate & Enter Shipping Mark (Freight, Invoice, FOB)

    Masukkan hasil perhitungan Freight, Invoice, FOB ke kolom **Shipping Mark**:

    Format Shipping Mark: "Freight: \$XXX.XX | Invoice: \$XXX.XX | FOB: \$XXX.XX"

    üßÆ Calculation Formulas (IMPORTANT ORDER):

    1. **FOB** = Net Weight (kg) $\times$ Harga Barang $\div 1000$
    2. **Invoice** = Freight $\div$ Dollar Rate (hari ini)
    3. **Freight** = Invoice - FOB
    üìä Access Excel Calculation Sheet ‚Üí

    13. Click "Add List" & Enter Product Details

    Tambahkan item produk dan verifikasi perhitungan sistem:

    • ‚úì **HS Code:** Dari material class database (Lihat Ref)
    • ‚úì **Net/Gross Weight:** Dari GA Remarks Info Form
    • ‚úì **Unit Price:** Dari SC yang diproses
    • ‚úì **Volume:** Panjang $\times$ Lebar $\times$ Tinggi $\div 1,000,000$
    • ‚úì **Quantity UOM:** Box/Roll/Sheet/Pack
    • Klik **CALCULATE** $\to$ **SAVE** $\to$ **POST e-Sample**

    14. Screenshot Invoice Number & Send to Document Team

    Setelah **POST e-Sample**, **Number Invoice** akan muncul di pojok atas.

    1. Ambil **Screenshot** Number Invoice yang muncul.
    2. Kirim **by email** ke **Team Dokumen**.

    Dokumen yang akan diproses Tim Dokumen: FOB, V-Legal, Packing List, COA, Invoice Approved.

    15. Send Reminder to GA for AWB Number

    Kirim reminder by Outlook kepada pihak GA untuk meminta **AWB (Air Waybill) Number**.

    Sertakan Invoice Number, Customer name, dan Destination Country.

    16. Receive AWB/DHL Tracking Number from GA

    Setelah itu mendapatkan informasi AWB Number atau DHL Tracking dari pihak GA. **Verify Information**.

    17. Enter AWB Number into e-Sample System & Notify Customer

    Login ke e-Sample, temukan sampel, dan berikan informasi AWB/DHL Tracking Number.

    Setelah itu, **Email Customer** dengan detail tracking link dan perkiraan waktu pengiriman.
