Local Sample Process Flow

Step-by-step workflow for local sample requests

1. Receive Email from Customer to IKK Customer Service

Customer service receives initial sample request email from customer with complete details.

Email Reception:

  • Customer sends email request to IKK Customer Service.
  • Review detailed information about required sample items.

Product Specifications:

  • Sample description and product type
  • **GSM** (Gram per Square Meter) specification
  • Grade/quality requirement
  • Quantity needed (number of pieces, kg, rolls, sheets)
  • Size/Format (A4 or custom dimensions)
  • **REMARKS** (special requirements, testing purpose, deadlines)

Recipient Information:

  • **ATTN** - Name of person receiving sample
  • Complete delivery address (street, city, postal code)
  • Contact email and phone number

Verify Information: Confirm all required details are complete. Acknowledge Receipt: Send confirmation email to customer.

2. Create SRF Form (Sample Request Form)

Fill out the complete SRF form with all required information from customer email.

SRF Field Details:

  • **SRF Date:** Enter the date of sample request creation (DD/MM/YYYY)
  • **Prepared By:** Enter the name of person creating the request
  • **Sample Description:** Provide detailed description of the sample product
  • **GSM:** Specify the paper/material weight
  • **Size/Format:** Enter dimensions (e.g., A4, custom size in cm)
  • **Remarks:** Add any special requirements or specifications
  • **Notes:** Include additional information if needed (quality notes, handling instructions)

üìã Recipient Details Section:

  • **ATTN (Attention):** Name of the person receiving the sample
  • **Recipient Address:** Complete delivery address with postal code
  • **Email Address:** Contact email for delivery notifications
  • **Phone Number:** Contact number for delivery coordination

3. Review and Verify SRF Information

Double-check all entered information for accuracy:

  • Verify all customer contact details are **correct**.
  • Confirm sample specifications (**GSM**, size, description).
  • Ensure recipient address is complete and **accurate**.
  • Check email and phone number format.
  • Review all remarks and notes for **clarity**.

4. Signature Collection

Obtain necessary approvals and signatures from key stakeholders.

  1. Submit form to **department head** for approval.
  2. Collect signature from **quality control manager**.
  3. Get final approval from **operations director**.
  4. Ensure all signatures are properly documented with dates.

5. Scan and Send to QC Team for Production

Forward the completed SRF to Quality Control team to produce the sample.

  • **Scan Document:** Scan the completed SRF form with all signatures in high resolution.
  • **Save as PDF:** Save with clear filename: `SRF_[Date]_[CustomerName].pdf`.
  • **Email to QC:** Send via email to QC team distribution list.
  • **Include Timeline:** Specify sample preparation timeline in email body.
  • **CC Stakeholders:** Copy relevant team members for transparency.
  • **Request Production:** QC team will produce the physical sample based on SRF specifications.

Wait for QC to complete sample production before next step.

6. QC Sample Production Complete

QC team produces the sample and prepares it for shipment.

  • **Sample Manufacturing:** QC produces sample according to SRF specifications.
  • **Quality Check:** QC verifies sample meets GSM, size, and quality requirements.
  • **Sample Packaging:** QC packages the sample appropriately (**A4 format** if specified).
  • **Production Complete:** QC confirms sample is ready for shipment.

Wait for production completion notification from QC team.

7. Request Information from General Affairs (GA)

After sample production is complete, get shipment details from GA team.

  • Contact GA Team: Notify GA that sample is ready for shipment.
  • Provide Sample Details: Share sample specifications and packaging info from QC.
  • Request Delivery Info: Get estimated delivery timeline.
  • Confirm Recipient: Verify ATTN name and complete recipient address with GA.
  • Delivery Method: Confirm preferred courier service (local courier, post, etc.).
  • Packaging Specs: Confirm A4 packaging or special packaging requirements.

Wait for GA confirmation before final coordination.

8. WhatsApp Notification to GA

Send immediate notification via **WhatsApp** for urgent coordination.

  • Notify GA Team: Send WhatsApp message to GA coordinator.
  • Sample Ready Alert: Confirm sample is ready for pickup from QC.
  • Key Details: Share Customer name, ATTN, Address, GSM, Size, Quantity.
  • Urgent Requirements: Mention any deadlines or special handling.
  • Confirm Pickup: Request pickup time from QC location.

9. Final GA Email Coordination

Send **formal email** to GA with complete shipment instructions.

  • ATTN & Address: Include complete recipient name and delivery address.
  • Contact Info: Provide recipient email and phone number.
  • Sample Details: Specify GSM, size, quantity, packaging type.
  • Delivery Timeline: Confirm expected delivery date.
  • Attach Documents: Include copy of **approved SRF form** and QC confirmation.

10. Track Shipment & Confirm Delivery

Monitor shipment and ensure successful delivery to customer within maximum **3-4 days**.

  • **Tracking Number:** Obtain tracking/receipt number from GA.
  • **Notify Customer:** Send tracking information to customer via email.
  • **Delivery Timeline:** Maximum pengiriman sample local **3-4 hari paling lama**.
  • **Monitor Status:** Track shipment progress daily until delivery.
  • **Follow Up:** If delivery exceeds 4 days, coordinate immediately with GA.
  • **Delivery Confirmation:** Follow up with customer to confirm sample received.
  • **Update Records:** Document delivery completion in system.
